Features and Terms
    -Dogpile - When people pile into a stream at the start
        -means game or stream is interesting

    -Weak End - Stream ending is significantly lower than average
        -means that stream is probably going too long or content is boring

    -Steady Growth - Content is good people are staying and watching
        -keep it up

    -Steady Decline - Content is okay, but weaker

    

    -Chat spike
        -Has more engagement 

    -Chat deadzone
        -No engagement, could be highly engaging, or you are boring
